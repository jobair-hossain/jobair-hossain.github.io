## 👨‍🏫 About

I am a Tenure-Track Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at the University of Central Arkansas (UCA). My research focuses on:

- Quantum Machine Learning (QML)
- Health Informatics: Multimodal Medical Data Analysis
- Cybersecurity: Malware and Ransomware Analysis and Software Supply Chain Vulnerabilities
- Software Engineering

This website presents my academic profile, including research, publications, teaching, and ongoing work.

---

## 🧪 Research Overview

My research integrates classical and quantum machine learning with real-world applications in healthcare and cybersecurity. Current directions include:

- Hybrid quantum-classical learning models
- Reinforcement learning-based adaptive model fusion
- Computational pathology using whole slide images (WSIs)
- Evidence-grounded generative AI for clinical decision support
- Secure and trustworthy AI systems
